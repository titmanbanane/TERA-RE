Our Services

A tailored offer, designed to adapt to each real estate project.

Transaction – Sale

A customized strategy to sell under the best conditions. We conduct a rigorous valuation based on the property's specifics and the local market. Each sale benefits from a personalized action plan.

Acquisition Consulting

Complete support, from search to signing. We study properties from all angles: legal, technical, tax, rental potential or appreciation. Whether you're buying to live or invest, we help you make an informed choice and secure your acquisition.

Rental Search

A turnkey solution for your rental project. We assist you with search, visits, lease review and file preparation. We also advise on guarantees to set up. A useful service for people on the move or short on time.

Market Analysis

A strategic evaluation of your property. We conduct an in-depth comparative study to position your property accurately, whether for sale, rental, or as part of inheritance, donation or wealth arbitration.

Real Estate Wealth Consulting

Solutions adapted to family and wealth issues. We intervene in complex contexts: joint ownership, transfer, donation, usufruct, asset valuation... Goal: protect your interests, enhance your assets and help you make the right long-term decisions.

Post-Acquisition Support

Follow-up after purchase to optimize your property. We remain by your side after acquisition: renovation advice, layout, decoration, rental or resale potential analysis. A valuable service to sustainably enhance your investment.
